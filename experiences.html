Come in as colleagues, Leave as Family!

For growth-oriented organizations, Gabriel sits with the leadership to design the coaching experiences that the team needs.

He focuses on two areas of experience which are

Building a High-Performance Team

Team building is not just about games, songs, and a campfire. It serves a larger purpose, which is realized when the team defines theirs with clarity. High-Performance Team Building is akin to a football game halftime talk. What will you do if you only have 10 minutes to motivate a team to win? Teams work together for years! Your halftime speech may last a little longer than 10 minutes, but the intensity and connection that it contains will drive you all. Make team-building a priority! Make team-building count.

The First Step

This is a 4 hours introductory session on knowing yourself and committing to being the best version of yourself. This is your every now and then “butt kick” or “Wake Up” experience every team needs.

For Educators

Education Superheroes

7 days a year, educators from around the world will assemble to redefine their mental construct and renew their purpose. We have the best vocation in the world and when that meets a vacation in Bali (that you really do need), it is heaven on earth. We are not going to listen to experts during these 7 days. We are going to teach because this is a place for practitioners. We are going to challenge ourselves to create the most fascinating classrooms with onlooking coaches and also the biggest judges; the students. Yes, you are going to inspire young minds in Bali. When you return home, you have the tools, emotional strength, support from fellow educators and a clear purpose to see you move forward.
